OUR INTRODUCTION

WELCOME TO SAFE PRO FIRE SERVICES PVT. LTD

Established in 2000, SAFE PRO FIRE SERVICES PVT. LTD. is a trusted and innovative manufacturer of portable, trolley/trailer mounted fire extinguishers and gas cartridges for commercial and industrial applications. We have earned a reputation for excellence in the fire protection industry, offering high-quality products and reliable services. With a fully equipped manufacturing plant in Bhiwandi, India. We have the capacity to produce over 10,000 units of fire extinguishers per day. Our commitment to staying at the forefront of best practices drives our continuous investment in technology and product development. All our products undergo rigorous in-house testing, with the option for third-party testing upon request. Our highly qualified management team and skilled engineers ensure the highest standards of quality control, supported by a modern laboratory. We are dedicated to providing excellent fire protection solutions that meet and exceed customer expectations, ensuring their satisfaction.
